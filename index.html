About HiMaC

Hindusthan Management Carnival (HiMaC) is an Annual intercollegiate management fest designed to showcase managerial talent through competitions which include business quizzes, case studies, marketing challenges, startup pitches, management games, and cultural performances. This carnival also provides a unique platform for students across various colleges to engage, compete, network, and learn.

About Department (MBA)

Department of Management Science was established in the year 2005, with a view to impart professional education in the field of business management. The MBA programme is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The college offers full time two years MBA Degree Programme.

A team of dedicated faculty members continuously monitor the corporate requirements and accordingly the students are trained in managerial skills, communication skills and leadership qualities. The faculty members with their extensive knowledge and industrial exposure strive to bring a real life experience into the classroom. The sanctioned intake of the MBA department is 120 students from the academic year 2024-2025 onwards.

Our students are well placed in corporates likes Oracle, JSW Steel, Swiggy, LeMeridien, CareerNet Consulting, Muthooot Finance, KGiSL, IndusInd Bank, Hexaware technologies, State bank of India, IBM, Parle, Udaan, BYJU, Canara bank, HCL, Reliance Retail, etc.

Emerge as a premier business school recognized globally for management education, practice and research that propels lifelong learning with ethical and professional values.

About HiCET

Hindusthan College of Engineering and Technology (HiCET) was established in the year 2000 by the great Industrialist and Philanthropist, Thiru.T.S.R.Khannaiyann, founder Chairman of Hindusthan Educational and Charitable Trust whose determination and dynamism made possible the realization of this institution of excellence. Surrounded with natures pristine beauty and an excellent infrastructure coupled with dedicated and experienced faculty has made the campus a much sought-after abode of learning.

HiCET is one of the premier technological institutions inculcating quality and value-based education through innovative teaching learning process for holistic development of the students.

At present, the college is offering 17 UG Programmes, 6 PG Programmes and 14 Research Programmes. Currently around 6000 students pursuing various Undergraduate Programmes (B.E./B.Tech.), Postgraduate Programmes (M.E./M.Tech, MBA & MCA) and Research Programs (Ph.D.) in the institution and are mentored by above 366 well qualified and experienced faculty members. The institution is recognized under Section 2(f) and 12B of University Grants Commission (UGC) and is an Autonomous Institution from 2016-17, affiliated to Anna University, Chennai with permanent affiliation for most of the programmes, approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). 86.6% of eligible programs are NBA accredited and our institution has been accredited by NAAC 'A++' Grade with CGPA of 3.69 out of 4 in Cycle 2 and Institution Ranked NIRF - India Rankings 2024 in Band 201-300 in Engineering category and Band 11-50 in Innovation category. Also an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution.

Many Industry-Integrated programmes and the Hinnovation Centre of the institution encourages creativity, innovation and risk acceptance, identifying business thereby encouraging entrepreneurship and start-ups.

The management also runs 12 other institutions in which 6 are higher-educational, which are highly rated by the society and accreditation and ranking bodies. With the very strong industrial experience for more than 40 years, the management also having 14 industries in the engineering domain such as transformers, control panels, switch gears, pipes, etc. and having 3 multi-specialty hospitals with each 350 beds in Coimbatore, HiCET nurtures future global leaders by imparting right knowledge, skills and building attitudes among students to face the world in a fresh, energetic and unrestrictive work environment, considering the integration of SDGs into the curriculum.

"Hindusthan College of Engineering and Technology march towards greatness by following the educational doctrines of our beloved leader, Dr APJ Abdul Kalam in transforming the youth of today to become leaders of tomorrow."

About HITECH

Hindusthan Institute of Technology (HITECH) was started in the year 2007 by the industrialist and philanthropist Thiru T.S.R. Khannaiyann. It is an Autonomous Institution focused on educating and preparing men and women for leadership in industry, government, and educational institutions; advancing the engineering professions' knowledge base; and influencing the future directions of engineering education and practice.

The college offers well-furnished classrooms, state-of-the-art laboratories, computer centres and a well-stocked library. Separate hostels with modern amenities are provided for men and women. The campus includes lush green lawns, playgrounds, a gym, facilities for indoor games and a fleet of vehicles to cater to transport needs.

HITECH emphasizes not only academic education but also character building with moral and ethical values. Since its inception, the college has provided world-class facilities and infrastructure for high-quality education and learning, focusing on transformational leadership, innovative training methodologies, and guiding students toward success.

The college library holds more than thirty thousand books and over 500 national & international journals, and it also hosts digital resources such as the IIT-NPTEL video library. Outdoor facilities for tennis, basketball and cricket are available, while the gymnasium and common rooms provide spaces for gymnastics, badminton, table tennis and other indoor games.
